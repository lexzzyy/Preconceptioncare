What is preconception care (PCC)?

Preconception care concept is a range of measures designed to promote the health of the expectant mother and her child, which, in order to be effective, must preferably be adopted prior to conception. It ensures that both the man and woman (teenagers, young adults or married couple) are in an optimal state of health before pregnancy and are also provided with some options that may not be available once pregnancy is confirmed whether before first or subsequent pregnancy thereby improving pregnancy-related outcomes. Preconception care interventions comprise a package of promotive, preventive, and curative health interventions that effectively improve maternal and child health.

Preconception Health for Women

Preconception health is important for every woman not just those planning a pregnancy. It means taking control and choosing healthy habits. It means living well, being healthy, and feeling good about life. . It focuses on taking steps now to protect the health of a baby they might have some time in the future and taking steps to get there.

Preconception Health for Men

Preconception health is important for men, too. It means choosing to get and stay as healthy as possible―and helping others to do the same as well. As a partner, it means encouraging and supporting your partner’s health. As a father, it means protecting your children. Preconception health is about providing yourself and your loved ones with a bright and healthy future. Taking care of your health now will help to ensure a better quality of life for yourself and your family in the coming years.

Advantages of Preconception Care

  • Fewer unwanted pregnancies
  • Improve outcome of pregnancy by decreasing mortality and morbidity in both mother and child
  • Shift from acute care to counselling-based preventive care

Barriers of Preconception Care

  • Medicalization of pregnancy
  • Not all pregnancies are planned
  • Understaff
  • Poor level of knowledge among health care providers
  • Health care professionals also often think they have too little time to provide PCC
  • Failure for reimbursement of PCC

About Preconception care

This App is a midwife-led preconception care intervention designed mainly for academic purpose to intimate female corp members about PCC in order to reduce maternal mortality and morbidity and improve birth outcomes.
