Preconception care

Preparing to have a baby can be challenging, regardless of the experience women still go through the process of getting pregnant without any medical consultation. This guide aims at educating intending couples in Ibadan metropolis on significance of preconception care thus enhancing the well-being of women of childbearing age in preparation for conception.

The way to go

This guide supports the integration of preconception care into women comprehensive care hence improving the health of women of reproductive age and their unborn child. It provides resources that address multifarious factors comprising nutririon, vitamin supplements, premarital genetic screening, vaccination and physical exercise.

Module I

PCC concept is a range of measures designed to promote the health of the expectant mother and her child, which, in order to be effective, must preferably be adopted prior to conception.
